Lemmatization or Stemming?

You want to normalize texts for the following 5 languages: Russian, English, Hungarian, Danish, and Bengali. Find the most appropriate way to do it. Your approach should:

  • Work fast, and
  • Produce good results (not valid words!)

Mark the languages that you would lemmatize rather than stem.

Mind that Russian and Bengali have 6 grammatical cases, English — 3 (just for pronouns), Hungarian — 18, Danish 2.
